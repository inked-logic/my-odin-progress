Oat Pancake

Description

Ingredients

  • Oat
  • Almonds
  • Walnuts
  • Hazelnuts
  • Cashews
  • Bananas
  • Milk

Recipe instructions

  1. Roast oat, almonds, walnuts, hazelnuts and cashews
  2. In a blender, put a glass of roasted oat
  3. Add some almonds, walnuts, hazelnuts and cashews
  4. Add one medium banana
  5. Scald some milk
  6. Add two to three ladles of scalded milk
  7. Blind the mix
  8. fill a pancake maker machine with the mix
  9. close the machine for 3 minutes
  10. Plate the pancake
