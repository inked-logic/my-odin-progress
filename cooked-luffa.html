Yassin's recipe for cooking luffa

Description

Ingredients

  • Luffa
  • Pumpkin
  • Cubanelle Peppers
  • Dried thyme
  • Salt
  • Rosemary herb
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic

Recipe instructions

  1. Prep the luffas, the pumpkin and the cubanelle peppers
  2. Cut them in small cubes
  3. Put them in a bowl together
  4. Add dried thyme, salt, rosemary herb and extra virgin olive oil
  5. Mix them together
  6. Put some baking paper into a baking sheet
  7. Fill the baking sheet with the mixture
  8. Add some garlics
  9. Put the baking sheet in a electric cooker for about 45 minutes
  10. Plate the dish
